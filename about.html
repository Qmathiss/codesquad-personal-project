About Sunsha Holistic Collective

The collective was established in 2020. While many people begin to look for natural alternatives to medicine sunsha collective was created. As a starting point to a spiritual journey into divine wellness. Sunsha Collective strides to educate and assist people in a aspects of holistic wellness. Here you will find books that assist in starting to heal yourself, as well as natural herbal remedies. If you are seeking asssitance in counseling the collective provides information on counselors within your area. As well as places to purchase wellness merchandise.
